[h1]Overview[/h1]

This mod adds new government names - which includes government titles such as "Kingdom" or "Duchy", ruler titles like "King" or "Duke", heir names, and consort names - to many cultures featured in Beyond Typus.

[b]Names[/b]
To see what names have been added, check the changelog or the Change Notes tab at the top of this page. However, some examples include:
[list]
[*]Names for Francien language group monarchies, such as "Royaume", "Roi", and "Reine"
[*]Names for Italian monarchies and republics
[*]Names for Spain, Castile, Aragon, and other Iberian monarchies
[*]Names for duchy-rank Flanders or any duchy-rank monarchy with Dutch or Flemish as its primary language and with its capital in the Flanders area
[/list]

[b]Requirements[/b]
[list]
[*]Beyond Typus
[/list]

[b]Compatibility[/b]
This mod should be compatible with [i]most[/i] mods, as the scope of it is very narrow.
Note also that existing games might have some issues if you enable this mod. Nothing catastrophic, just some names might not update.
